# Cookie Policy

**Effective date:** 13 April 2026 — Version 1.0

This policy explains what cookies and similar technologies SuperBad Lite uses, why, and how you can control them.

---

## 1. What's a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file stored on your device by your browser. We also use `localStorage` (a similar browser storage mechanism) for some preferences.

---

## 2. What we use and why

### Strictly necessary

These are required for the platform to work. You can't opt out of them.

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `sb_session` | Keeps you signed in during your session | Session |
| `next-auth.session-token` | Admin authentication (NextAuth) | 30 days |
| `sb_portal_session` | Client portal access | 90 days |
| `sb_cookie_consent` | Remembers your cookie choice (localStorage) | 1 year |

### Functionality

Optional but useful — remember your display preferences (theme, motion, sound settings).

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| `sb_preset_theme` | Display theme preference | 1 year |
| `sb_preset_typeface` | Typeface preference | 1 year |

### Analytics

Optional — error monitoring via Sentry. Helps us fix bugs faster.

| Name | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Sentry session | Client-side error and performance data | Session |

No advertising or tracking cookies. No Google Analytics. No Facebook Pixel.

---

## 3. Third-party cookies

Stripe may set cookies during payment flows. These are governed by [Stripe's Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/au/privacy).

---

## 4. How to manage cookies

**Via our banner (EU visitors):** If you see the cookie consent banner, you can accept all, reject all, or manage individual categories.

**Via your browser:** You can delete or block cookies through your browser settings. Note that blocking strictly necessary cookies will break core functionality.

**Opt-out links:**
- Sentry: [sentry.io/privacy](https://sentry.io/privacy) — opt-out via Sentry's privacy controls.

---

## 5. Your rights

If you're in the EU/UK, the GDPR gives you the right to withdraw consent at any time. Clear `sb_cookie_consent` from your browser's `localStorage` and reload — the consent banner will reappear.

If you're in Australia, see our [Privacy Policy](/lite/legal/privacy) for your rights under the Privacy Act 1988.

---

## 6. Changes

We'll update the effective date above when this policy changes. If we add new cookies, we'll update this page and (where required) seek fresh consent.
