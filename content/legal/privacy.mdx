# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 13 April 2026 — Version 1.0

SuperBad Marketing ABN 00 000 000 000 ("SuperBad", "we", "us") is committed to handling your personal information responsibly. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what your rights are.

We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

---

## 1. Who this applies to

This policy applies to:
- Prospective and current clients of SuperBad Marketing.
- Users of the SuperBad Lite platform (portal and admin surfaces).
- Anyone who emails us or contacts us via our website.

---

## 2. What we collect

| Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Identity** | Name, business name, ABN |
| **Contact** | Email address, phone number, physical address |
| **Business** | Project briefs, brand materials, photography/video deliverables |
| **Billing** | Invoice history, payment status (we do not store card numbers — Stripe holds those) |
| **Platform usage** | Pages visited, actions taken within the portal, timestamps |
| **Technical** | Browser type, IP address, session cookies |

We collect only what we need to deliver and improve our services.

---

## 3. How we use your information

- **Service delivery** — shoot coordination, deliverables, invoicing, portal access.
- **Communication** — follow-ups, project updates, billing notices.
- **Platform improvement** — error monitoring, usage patterns (no third-party ad tracking).
- **Legal compliance** — record-keeping as required by Australian tax law (ATO 7-year retention).

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use it for advertising.

---

## 4. Who we share it with

| Recipient | Why |
|---|---|
| **Stripe** | Payment processing |
| **Resend** | Transactional email delivery |
| **Anthropic** | AI-assisted drafts and analysis (anonymised where possible) |
| **Sentry** | Error monitoring (technical logs only) |
| **Cloudflare R2** | Encrypted database backups |

All third parties are contractually required to handle your data appropriately. We don't share with anyone else without your consent.

---

## 5. Storage and security

Your data is stored on servers located in Australia (or in Cloudflare's nearest data centre). We use AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive credentials. Access to the platform requires authentication.

We retain records for 7 years as required by ATO guidelines. Portal access and non-active data may be archived earlier.

---

## 6. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences. If you're visiting from the EU, you'll see a cookie consent banner. See our [Cookie Policy](/lite/legal/cookie-policy) for details.

---

## 7. Your rights (Australian Privacy Principles)

Under the APPs you have the right to:
- **Access** personal information we hold about you.
- **Correct** inaccurate or out-of-date information.
- **Request deletion** (subject to our legal retention obligations).
- **Complain** to us or to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe we've mishandled your data.

---

## 8. Data subject requests (DSR)

To exercise any of the above rights, email us at [privacy@superbadmedia.com.au](mailto:privacy@superbadmedia.com.au).

We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days and respond within **30 days**.

---

## 9. Changes to this policy

We'll update the effective date above when this policy changes materially. We'll notify active clients by email if the changes are significant.
