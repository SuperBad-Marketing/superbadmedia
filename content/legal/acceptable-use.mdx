# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective date:** 13 April 2026 — Version 1.0

This policy sets out what you can and can't do when accessing the SuperBad Lite platform or receiving services from SuperBad Marketing.

---

## 1. What's fine

- Accessing areas of the platform you've been explicitly given access to.
- Uploading briefs, assets, and feedback relating to your project.
- Communicating with us through the portal, email, or phone.
- Downloading your own deliverables and reports.

---

## 2. What's not fine

**Don't:**

- Access, attempt to access, or probe any area you haven't been granted permission for.
- Share your portal link or credentials with anyone else.
- Use the platform to store, transmit, or display content that is illegal, defamatory, or harmful.
- Attempt to reverse-engineer, scrape, or copy the platform.
- Use automated tools (bots, scripts, crawlers) to interact with the platform.
- Interfere with the security or performance of the platform or its underlying infrastructure.
- Impersonate another user or misrepresent your identity.

---

## 3. Consequences

If you breach this policy:

- We may suspend or terminate your access, without notice in serious cases.
- We may invoice you for any costs your breach causes us.
- We may take legal action if required.

---

## 4. Reporting a breach

If you become aware of a breach of this policy — including if your account or link has been compromised — please contact us immediately at [hello@superbadmedia.com.au](mailto:hello@superbadmedia.com.au).

---

## 5. Changes

We may update this policy. Updates take effect when we post the new version with a revised effective date.
