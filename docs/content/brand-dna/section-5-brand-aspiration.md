# Section 5 — Brand Aspiration

**Domain:** `aspiration` (primary), with cross-domain personality and values tags.
**Format:** Mixed — preference and scenario. No visual questions.
**Measures:** How they want their business to feel to others, the gap between current and ideal, motivational drives, legacy, power, belonging.
**Spec ref:** `docs/specs/brand-dna-assessment.md` §4.1.

The closing section. By now the person has revealed aesthetic taste, communication wiring, core values, and creative instincts. This section asks them to look forward — not at what they are, but at what they're reaching for. The contrast between this section and the earlier ones is where the most powerful profile insights live.

No between-section insight follows Section 5 — the next moment is the cinematic reveal.
