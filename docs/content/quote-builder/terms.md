### The commitment

Your quote includes a commitment period — the number of months you and
SuperBad agree to work together. The commitment starts on the day you
accept and pay.

During the commitment period, SuperBad delivers the scope described in
your quote. You pay the agreed amount each month (or as a one-off for
project work).

After the commitment period ends, your retainer continues month-to-month.
You can change, upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time with no
penalties.

---

### What you pay

The total on your quote is GST-inclusive. That's the number — no hidden
extras, no escalation clauses, no "plus GST" surprises.

**Retainer quotes:** You pay a monthly amount, charged on the same day
each month. Your first payment happens when you accept the quote.

**Project quotes:** You pay once when you accept the quote. That's it.

**Mixed quotes (retainer + one-off items):** Your first payment covers
the one-off items plus the first month's retainer. Monthly charges
continue from there.

Tax invoices are issued for every payment. Stripe-billed clients receive
Stripe receipts; manually-billed clients receive invoices from SuperBad
with full ATO-compliant details.

---

### If you want to leave early

We'd rather you stayed, but we won't trap you. If your commitment
period hasn't ended yet, you have three options:

**1. Let's talk about it.** No charge. Andy reaches out within 24 hours
for a short call. After the call, the other two options are still
available if you want them.

**2. Pay the remaining months.** Your outstanding commitment is charged
to your card on file, and your retainer ends immediately. No further
deliverables after that day.

**3. Buyout at 50%.** Half the remaining commitment is charged to your
card on file, and your retainer ends immediately. No further
deliverables after that day.

All three options are available from your account portal at any time
during the commitment period.

---

### Pausing (subscription products only)

If you have a subscription product with SuperBad, you can pause it once
per commitment period for up to one month. During the pause:

- No charges are made
- Your commitment end date extends by one month
- Your data and settings are preserved
- You'll receive a heads-up 3 days before the subscription resumes

One pause per commitment period. After that, the other exit options
apply.

---

### After the commitment ends

Your retainer continues month-to-month. You can:

- **Upgrade** to a higher-tier retainer
- **Downgrade** to a lower-tier retainer
- **Cancel** with no penalty — subscription ends at the current billing
  period's close

No buyouts, no fees, no conversations required. You manage it from your
account portal.

---

### What SuperBad commits to

- Deliver the scope described in your quote, on the cadence described
- Invoice accurately and on time
- Be reachable and responsive
- Not change pricing during your commitment period
- Process any cancellation or exit promptly and without obstruction

If SuperBad fails to deliver the agreed scope, you can raise it with
Andy directly. If it can't be resolved, the commitment can be ended
early at SuperBad's expense — no charge to you.
